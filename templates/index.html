Quick statistics & guidance

~ 537M people

Estimated global number of adults living with diabetes (WHO, approx.).

Annual screening

Adults over 45 or with risk factors: consider yearly glucose screening and risk assessment.

Modifiable risks

Diet, physical activity, BMI and smoking are key modifiable factors to reduce risk.

Precautions & actionable tips

Routine monitoring
Regularly check fasting glucose/HbA1c if you have risk factors (age, family history, high BMI).
Healthy lifestyle
Adopt a balanced diet, increase physical activity and maintain healthy weight to lower risk.
Medication & follow-up
If diagnosed, follow clinician guidance on medication, monitoring and complications screening.
When to seek care
Severe thirst, unexplained weight loss, frequent urination, blurred vision — seek medical care promptly.
