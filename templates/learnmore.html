What is Diabetes?

Diabetes is a chronic medical condition that affects how your body converts food into energy. It occurs when the pancreas does not produce enough insulin or when the body cannot effectively use the insulin it produces.

Early detection is extremely important because untreated diabetes can lead to heart issues, kidney damage, nerve problems, and vision loss.

How Machine Learning Predicts Diabetes

Machine Learning models analyze medical inputs such as glucose levels, BMI, blood pressure, and insulin values to predict whether a person is likely to have diabetes.

  • ✔ Logistic Regression
  • ✔ Random Forest Classifier
  • ✔ Support Vector Machine (SVM)
  • ✔ Decision Trees
ML learns from thousands of patient records to identify patterns that doctors may miss.

Common Symptoms of Diabetes

  • Frequent urination
  • Excessive thirst or hunger
  • Fatigue
  • Sudden weight loss
  • Slow wound healing

How to Reduce Diabetes Risk

  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Eat a balanced diet
  • Exercise regularly
  • Reduce sugar intake
  • Have regular medical checkups
